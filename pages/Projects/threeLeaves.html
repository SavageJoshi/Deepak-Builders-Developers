About Three Leaves

Embrace luxury living at its finest with Three Leaves, the signature project of DBD, and the blockbuster project of Nashik, boasting 33 best-in-class amenities. Situated in the upcoming hub of Govind Nagar, this residential masterpiece is located in the heart of the city yet offers a calm and peaceful environment. Offering 3, 4 and 5 BHK apartments, nestled within this project spanning 32 floors and three podiums, Three Leaves personifies opulence and elegance. Make Three Leaves your new destination for luxury living.
