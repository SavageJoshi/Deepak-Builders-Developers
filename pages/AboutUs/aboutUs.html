Deepak Builders
& Developers

Deepak Builders and Developers (DBD) is a leading player in Nashik's real estate industry, proudly boasting over 100 successful residential and commercial projects spanning 98 million sq. ft. Our goal is to create ultra-premium, ultra-spacious properties with stunning architectural elevations. Celebrating 35 years of excellence, we have consistently delivered high-quality homes and commercial spaces, ensuring safety, happiness, and well-being for every family in our projects. We prioritize punctuality and foster a peaceful living environment, distinguished by innovative construction technology, modern machinery, and a customer-centric approach. Despite challenges like Demonetization, GST implementation, and the COVID-19 pandemic, our project deliveries have remained steadfast, and the company has stayed stable and reliable, adapting with resilience to evolving circumstances.

Our Story

1989

Initiation & foundation of Deepak Builders & Developers.

2001

Extended our expertise towards commercial sector, along with residential projects.

2007

Entered hospitality sector by constructing Hotel Sai Leela, Sai Baba guest house and so on.

2009

Completed iconic Star Zone commercial complex in Nasik Road, admeasuring around 2,50,000 sq. ft. This project also houses the first ever Passport office of North Maharashtra.

2013

Constructed luxurious Hotel Shalimar Inn, Hotel Panchvati Pride, The Square, first eye Hospital in Panchavati Anand Sankul premises.

2021

Associated with Tata’s IHCL to construct the first ever Hotel Vivanta in Nashik

2023

MOU with Westside, Star Bazar & Zudio at multiple locations across our projects. MOU with Haldiram’s group to open 5 stores in Nashik

Working Sectors

Commercial

We specialize in constructing commercial buildings that cater to the dynamic needs of businesses in Nashik. Known for impressive elevations, spacious showrooms, shops, and offices with unique architectural designs, our projects have earned a trusted reputation. Hosting Nashik's passport office and over 80 world-renowned brands, we are the preferred choice for top-notch commercial hubs that foster productivity and growth.

Residential

At our company, we deliver exceptional residential projects tailored to diverse client needs. With over 50 completed projects, we offer spacious flats and housing with state-of-the-art amenities, unmatched in Nashik. Our flagship skyscrapers, Three Leaves and Star Apex, are set to be Nashik's finest. Resident satisfaction and consistent delivery of commitments have earned us a trusted reputation, making us a preferred choice in the residential sector.

Healthcare

We are dedicated to constructing top-tier healthcare facilities, including state-of-the-art hospitals, clinics, and wellness centres in Nashik. Our standout project, Star Plus B, is a comprehensive healthcare hub with over 30 doctors and numerous renowned hospitals offering a full spectrum of services from CT scans to lab tests, with stretcher lifts for patient convenience. Additionally, we launched Nashik's first animal ambulance and will soon open Elsa, an advanced animal hospital.

Hospitality

In the hospitality sector, we deliver projects combining luxury, comfort, and functionality. As Nashik's first developer to partner with TATA Indian Hotels Company Limited (IHCL), we are introducing the upscale Vivanta hotel, featuring world-class amenities, elegant interiors, and exceptional service for business and leisure travellers. This partnership marks a milestone in our commitment to excellence. Additionally, our diverse hotel portfolio in Nashik caters to both luxury and budget-friendly needs. We are poised for further expansion, continuing to set new standards in hospitality.

Infrastructure

We are expanding in the infrastructure sector to contribute to Nashik's development with innovative, sustainable projects. Our Ready-Mix Concrete (RMC) plant provides high-quality materials for our projects. Committed to cutting-edge engineering and stringent quality standards, our goal is to enhance urban living with robust infrastructure that benefits the community.

Retail

We develop retail spaces that enhance shopping experiences and provide businesses with prime locations. Our vibrant, well-designed complexes attract foot traffic with a mix of stores, restaurants, and entertainment options. Known for strategic locations, ample parking, and modern infrastructure, our projects become bustling hubs in Nashik. Retail giants like Zudio, Westside, and Haldiram’s have chosen our spaces, highlighting our reputation as the go-to developer for premier retail environments.
