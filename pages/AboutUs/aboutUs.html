About Us

Deepak Builders and Developers is a progressive real estate and development company that is committed to bringing modern concepts, sophisticated design and the latest techniques in construction to the people of Nashik. The DBD brand has long been synonymous with transparency and customer-centricity since 35 years.
Under our visionary leader, director, Mr. Deepak Chande, DBD has carved an important place in Nashik’s skyline over the last three decades. Each project is an example of consistency, quality and perfection. We seek to exceed customer expectations, always.

Our Story

1989

Mr. Deepak Chande begins his journey in construction and real estate with Deepak Builders and Developers.

1990 - 2000

DBD focuses on various residential projects.

2001 - 2007

DBD adds a new feather to its hat with a shift in focus toward commercial projects.

2007 - 2009

DBD enters the hospitality sector by successfully completing the construction and handover of Hotel Sai Leela, Sai Baba Guest House, and other projects.

2009

DBD finishes work on Star Zone Commercial Complex, a project measuring 2,50,000 sq. ft. This location is home to the first passport office in western Maharashtra.

2013

Shalimar Inn, Hotel Panchvati Pride and The Square: Hospitality projects concluded by DBD.

2015

Finished construction on the first-ever eye hospital on the premises of Panchvati Anand Sankul.

2016 - 2020

DBD builds Star Hospital and Commercial Complex on Nashik Road, measuring 2,60,000 sq. ft. Also finished construction for Mauli Medical Science Pvt Ltd, with a capacity of 50 beds.

2023

DBD signs MoU with Haldiram Group to open 5 stores across Nashik city.

Vision

Our mission extends beyond mere construction; it's about crafting architectural marvels that transcend boundaries, offering unparalleled experiences that captivate the senses. By weaving innovation, sustainability, and cultural richness into each project, we aim to not only redefine perfection but also to showcase Nashik's potential on a global scale. Through our commitment to excellence, we aspire to create spaces that resonate with people worldwide, putting Nashik firmly on the map as a hub of creativity and ingenuity.

Mission

The DBD Group is committed to enriching lives through exceptional living and working spaces, guided by ethical and equitable policies across all organizational levels. Our projects are fueled by innovation and ambition, striving for outstanding results that surpass expectations. With a focus on phenomenal design, we aim to contribute value to Nashik's infrastructure landscape, leaving a lasting mark on the city's development. Our mission is not just about constructing buildings but creating environments that elevate the quality of life and reflect our dedication to excellence in every aspect.
