Deepak Builders
& Developers

Deepak Builders and Developers (DBD) is a leading player in Nashik's real estate industry, proudly boasting over 100 successful residential and commercial projects spanning 98 million sq. ft. Our goal is to create ultra-premium, ultra-spacious properties with stunning architectural elevations. Celebrating 35 years of excellence, we have consistently delivered high-quality homes and commercial spaces, ensuring safety, happiness, and well-being for every family in our projects. We prioritize punctuality and foster a peaceful living environment, distinguished by innovative construction technology, modern machinery, and a customer-centric approach. Despite challenges like Demonetization, GST implementation, and the COVID-19 pandemic, our project deliveries have remained steadfast, and the company has stayed stable and reliable, adapting with resilience to evolving circumstances.

Mr. Deepak Kalyanji Chande

CEO, Founder & Proprietor

When I established Deepak Builders and Developers (DBD) in 1989, my vision was to transform Nashik’s skyline. Every time we take up a project, we pay attention to the smallest detail to ensure that every aspect of the home or office we are building is simply perfect. Our dedication to excellence in the real estate industry has always been based on a vision that goes much beyond physical structures. Since inception, I have ensured that our company’s projects put our customers above all. Over the years, we have witnessed the landscape of Nashik evolve, and it fills us with pride to have played a part in this transformation. In next 5 years, we plan to bring some ambitious projects to fruition. DBD Group will always be with you, whether you are looking for a new home for your family or an ultra-modern workspace for your business.

Our Story

1989

Initiation & foundation of Deepak Builders & Developers.

2001

Extended our expertise towards commercial sector, along with residential projects.

2007

Entered hospitality sector by constructing Hotel Sai Leela, Sai Baba guest house and so on.

2009

Completed iconic Star Zone commercial complex in Nasik Road, admeasuring around 2,50,000 sq. ft. This project also houses the first ever Passport office of North Maharashtra.

2013

Constructed luxurious Hotel Shalimar Inn, Hotel Panchvati Pride, The Square, first eye Hospital in Panchavati Anand Sankul premises.

2013

Completed Star Plus hospital & commercial complex in Nashik Road, admeasuring 2,60,000 sq. ft. Constructed another super specialty hospital, Mauli Medical Science Pvt. Ltd, with a capacity of 50 Beds

2021

Associated with Tata’s IHCL to construct the first ever Hotel Vivanta in Nashik

2023

MOU with Westside, Star Bazar & Zudio at multiple locations across our projects. MOU with Haldiram’s group to open 5 stores in Nashik

Working Sectors

Commercial

The epidemic caused a short decline in demand, but in FY2022, it rebounded. Employees returning to their work was the reason for this recovery's notable resilience. Office spaces are getting larger post-pandemic to meet the needs of social separation for worker well-being. Our unique office designs, together with tech-enabled asset services, are contributing to the future-proofing of our services.

Residential

For decades, our projects have gone on to define and redefine the meaning of housing and lifestyles for generations. Our carefully crafted, smartly designed and aesthetically curated residential units help our customers in turning spaces into homes. This, along with our consistent delivery of commitments, have allowed us to develop a trusted reputation among our clients. Our residential product line comprises townships, apartments, mansions, luxury villas, row houses, plotted development, and affordable housing.

Healthcare

We understand the unique needs and regulations governing healthcare real estate, offering comprehensive services ranging from site selection and development to property management and leasing. We collaborate closely with healthcare providers to create efficient and functional spaces that meet specific medical requirements while optimizing patient care and operational effectiveness.

Hospitality

People are willing to explore and take vacations with newfound vigor after two years of travel restrictions. Staycations are growing in popularity as a result of the workplace's increased flexibility, which is fueling the industry's expansion. As a player in this market, customers now choose our elegant hotels, resorts, and serviced flats above competitors' offerings in a number of areas.

Infrastructure

Welcome to the foundation of modern living, where infrastructure intertwines with opportunity to create vibrant communities. Our portfolio of infrastructure projects embodies a commitment to connectivity, sustainability, and quality of life. From transportation networks that seamlessly link neighborhoods to utilities and communication systems that power everyday life, each project is a testament to our dedication to shaping the cities of tomorrow. With a focus on innovation and resilience, our infrastructure developments not only meet the needs of today but also pave the way for a sustainable and prosperous future.

Retail

In FY2022, customer traffic in Indian retail malls quickly revived. As the pandemic's impacts began to fade, consumer sentiment improved, which swiftly translated into higher sales and occupancy rates. Finally, people are leaving their homes in order to treat themselves, which is releasing stored-up demand. As a result, people increasingly opt to shop and socialize in our malls.
